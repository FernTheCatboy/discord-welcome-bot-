Welcome to the primary space for the main 2 divisions of Legionary Vostannia: The Combined Arms Battalion and Ehrenwache

The Combined Arms Battalion [CAB] battles in air, land and sea, emphasizing modern and 20th century combat, comprising 5 companies representing various combat disciplines, and participating in games like Dead Ahead, MTC4 and TBG3.
