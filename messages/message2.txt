Welcome, Legionare!

This is the main hub for the 2 main division of Legionary Vostannia, the Combined Arms Battalion and Ehrenwache

The Combined Arms Battalion [CAB] operates across air, land and sea, specializing and leading the field in contemporary and 20th century warfare, with 4 companies dedicated to different combat approaches, competing in titles such as Dead Ahead, MTC4 and TBG3.
You can learn more on [CAB's official website](https://combinedarmsbattalion.com) or in the dedicated discord channel. https://discord.com/channels/984714357474734111/1029862984975134720

Ehrenwache stands at the forefront of Vostannia, serving as its bastion with its title of Honor Guard. Be it line battles or trench warfare, the Guard has yet to break ranks– even if defeat is nigh. Other forces may get routed, but our division ensures that every loss is a pyrrhic victory for the other side. 
You won't have to worry about that. When it comes to battles, they win 12 battles per loss. If they only lost 22 times, how many wins do you they have? If you're interested in leaving your mark in our battle records, find your local **Adjutant** and send a message in https://discord.com/channels/984714357474734111/1029861765334437888 so we can help you with your onboarding and questions.
