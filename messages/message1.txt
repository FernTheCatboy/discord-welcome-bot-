Hello and welcome to the First Vostannian Legion!

This is the primary server for the Combined Arms Battalion and Ehrenwache, which act as the Legions main 2 divisions.

The Combined Arms Battalion [CAB] fights in air, land and sea, focusing on modern and 20th century warfare, having 4 companies for the different combat styles, and playing games such as Dead Ahead, MTC4 and TBG3, they are leading the combined warfare scene with unrivalled levels of skill and strategy.
You can find more info at [CAB's official site](https://collapseproject.uk) or in the dedicated discord channel. https://discord.com/channels/984714357474734111/1029862984975134720

Ehrenwache (Honor Guard) is Legionary Vostannia's first line of defense, an infantry division well-versed in Napoleonic & ACS warfare. With over 270 victories under the Guard's name, they continue to relentlessly advance in games like Waterloo at home, Liberation, Windward, Vestfyn, conquering land under Vostannia's banner. 
For more information on enlistment and on the Guard as a whole, contact an **Adjutant** or send a message in https://discord.com/channels/984714357474734111/1029861765334437888.

Please note that you have a week as of receiving this message to enlist in a division or you will be kicked from the server.
